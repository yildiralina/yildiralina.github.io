Exercises

Prior to each workout, complete a 5- to 10-minute Warm-Up.

Exercises Sets Reps Rest
Hand Crossover 1 5–10 0

Two days a week, alternate between Weight Workout A and Weight Workout B, resting for at least a day after each session.

Weight Workout A
Exercises Sets Reps Rest
1. Side Plank 3 30–60 sec 1 min
2A. Single-Leg Barbell Straight-Leg Deadlift 3 12 1 min
2B. Dumbbell Row 3 12 1 min
3A. Dumbbell Squat 3 12 1 min
3B. Dumbbell Bench Press 3 12 1 min
Perform Exercise 1 as a straight set. That is, complete all of your sets of that movement before moving on to Exercise 2A. Rest for 1 minute after each set.
Perform Exercises 2A and 2B as a pair. Do one set of Exercise 2A, rest for 1 minute, then do one set of Exercise 2B. Rest for 1 minute again, then repeat until you've completed all three sets of both exercises.
Perform Exercises 3A and 3B as a pair. Do one set of Exercise 3A, rest for 1 minute, then do one set of Exercise 3B. Rest for 1 minute again, then repeat until you've completed all three sets of both exercises.
Weight Workout B
Exercises Sets Reps Rest
1. Side Plank 3 30–60 sec 1 min
2A. Dumbbell Bulgarian Split Squat 3 12 1 min
2B. EZ-Bar Pullover 3 12 1 min
3A. Barbell Deadlift 3 12 1 min
3B. Dumbbell Shoulder Press 3 12 1 min
Perform Exercise 1 as a straight set. That is, complete all of your sets of that movement before moving on to Exercise 2A. Rest for 1 minute after each set.
Perform Exercises 2A and 2B as a pair. Do one set of Exercise 2A, rest for 1 minute, then do one set of Exercise 2B. Rest for 1 minute again, then repeat until you've completed all three sets of both exercises.
Perform Exercises 3A and 3B as a pair. Do one set of Exercise 3A, rest for 1 minute, then do one set of Exercise 3B. Rest for 1 minute again, then repeat until you've completed all three sets of both exercises.

Do the Cardio Workout immediately after each Weight Workout.

Cardio Workout
Exercises Sets Reps Rest
Body-Weight Squat 2 24 0
Body-Weight Alternating Lunge 2 12 0
Body-Weight Split Jump 2 12 0
Body-Weight Jump Squat 2 24 4–5 min
Do one set of each exercise without resting, and keep track of how long it takes to complete the circuit. Then rest for twice that duration, and repeat once. When you can finish the circuit in 90 seconds, skip the rest.

Exercise Blueprint

Alternate between the Weight Workout A, the Cardio Workout, and the Weight Workout B three days a week—complete the Warm-Up before each workout.

Exercises Sets Reps Rest
Hand Crossover 1 10 0

Walking Heel to Butt 1 10 0
Walking High Knees 1 10 0

Jumping Jacks 1 10 0
Low Side-to-Side Lunge 1 10 0
Body-Weight Squat 1 10 0
Reverse Lunge with Reach Back 1 10 0
Weight Workout A
Exercises Sets Reps Rest
Dumbbell Squat, quadriceps 3 15 1 min
Dumbbell Row, upper back 3 15 1 min
Single-Leg Barbell Straight-Leg Deadlift, glutes & hamstrings 3 15/leg 1 min
Dumbbell Bench Press, chest 3 15 1 min
Side Plank, core 3 30–60 sec 1 min
Perform a set of Dumbbell Squats, rest for 1 minute, then do a set of Dumbbell Rows. Rest for another minute and repeat until you've completed three sets of both exercises. Next, execute a set of Single-Leg Barbell Straight-Leg Deadlifts, rest for 1 minute, and then proceed with a set of Dumbbell Bench Presses. Rest for another minute and continue this pattern until all three sets of both exercises are done. Finally, finish with three sets of the Side Plank, resting for 1 minute after each set.
Weight Workout B
Exercises Sets Reps Rest
Dumbbell Bulgarian Split Squat, quadriceps 3 15/leg 1 min
EZ-Bar Pullover, lats 3 15 1 min
Barbell Deadlift, glutes & hamstrings 3 15 1 min
Dumbbell Shoulder Press, shoulders 3 15 1 min
Side Plank, core 3 30–60 sec 1 min
Perform a set of Dumbbell Bulgarian Split Squats, rest for 1 minute, then do a set of EZ-Bar Pullovers. Rest for another minute and repeat until you've completed three sets of both exercises. Next, execute a set of Barbell Deadlifts, rest for 1 minute, and then proceed with a set of Dumbbell Shoulder Presses. Rest for another minute and continue this pattern until all three sets of both exercises are done. Finally, finish with three sets of the Side Plank, resting for 1 minute after each set.
Cardio Workout
Exercises Sets Reps Rest
Body-Weight Squat 2 24 1 min
Body-Weight Alternating Reverse Lunge 2 12/leg 1 min
Body-Weight Split Jump 2 12/leg 1–2 min
Body-Weight Jump Squat 2 24 1–2 min
Side-Lying Thoracic Rotation 1 10 0
Perform two sets of Body-Weight Squats with a 1-minute rest between. Follow with two sets of Body-Weight Alternating Reverse Lunges, pausing for 1 minute between. Next, do two sets of Body-Weight Split Jumps, resting 1–2 minutes after each. Then, two sets of Body-Weight Jump Squats, with a 1–2 minute break in between. Conclude with Side-Lying Thoracic Rotations.
